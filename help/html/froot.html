How to Play Froot

"What do I have to do in this game?"
The goal of the game is to clear the playing field of all fruits.

"And how do I do that?"
You control the little launch pad at the bottom of the screen with the left and right buttons. Use it to aim and shoot the fruit in the launch pad upwards using the center button. If the launched fruit hits two or more fruits of the same kind they disappear. Other fruits that were connected only to the disappearing fruits will be knocked off.

"Ok. But what's with the fancy yellow bar on the right screen edge?"
The bar fills up with each fruit you shoot. If it is full the ceiling will move down one step and push all the fruits on the playing field with it. If the fruits stack too far down the game is over. Knocking off some fruits will lower the bar and thus slow down the descent of the ceiling.

"Uh. All that sounds kind of weird and complicated!"
If you think these instructions are complicated and confusing then just go ahead and play the game. It's all very simple once you actually try it.

"Help! I can't get rid of the lemons!"
The lemons that show up in higher difficulty levels cannot be removed directly because you will never get a lemon for the launcher. Knock them off by removing the fruits above them instead.

"Is there anything else I should know?"
  • You can abort a running game by pressing the "Cancel" button.
  • You can change the difficulty level on the main screen. The high score list shows the scores for the selected difficulty level.
  • High scores can be reset through the options dialog which is accessible through the main screen.
  • Sound is disabled in this version of the game.
